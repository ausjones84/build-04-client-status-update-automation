- n8n workflow configuration (stage change trigger + weekly check-in)

- Webhook integration between GHL and n8n
- SMS and email template customization (up to 9 stages)
- Attorney escalation notification setup
- End-to-end testing and QA
- 30-day post-launch support
- Documentation handoff

---

## Monthly Retainer Includes ($397/month)

- n8n workflow monitoring and uptime checks
- GHL API updates and compatibility maintenance
- Message template updates (up to 2 revisions/month)
- Addition of new pipeline stages as needed
- Monthly performance report (# of automated messages sent, delivery rates)
- Priority support response (within 24 hours)

## ROI Analysis

### Time Saved Per Month

| Activity | Without Automation | With Automation | Time Saved |
|----------|-------------------|-----------------|------------|
| Status call handling | 15 min x 20 calls = 5 hrs | Calls reduced 40-60% = 2 hrs | 3 hrs/month |
| Manual update emails | 5 min x 30 updates = 2.5 hrs | Fully automated | 2.5 hrs/month |
| Weekly check-in calls | 10 min x 10 clients = 1.67 hrs | Automated SMS | 1.67 hrs/month |

**Estimated time saved: 7+ hours/month**

### Revenue Impact

At $250/hr attorney time: 7 hrs x $250 = **$1,750 value/month**  
Monthly retainer: $397  
**Net monthly value: $1,353**

Payback period on setup fee: **< 1 month**

## Scope Limitations

The following are NOT included in the base pricing:

- Custom CRM integrations beyond GHL
- More than 9 pipeline stages (add $150/stage)
- Multi-language SMS templates (add $300)
- Custom reporting dashboards (quoted separately)
- White-labeling for resale (contact for licensing)
